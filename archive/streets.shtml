Street Networks

Last graph file update: Mar 28th, 2011

The graphs Asia, Belgium, Europe, Germany, Great-Britain, Italy, Luxemburg and Netherlands are (roughly speaking) undirected and unweighted versions of the largest strongly connected component of the corresponding Open Street Map road networks. The Open Street Map road networks have been taken from http://download.geofabrik.de.

Detailed information on how the graphs have been converted can be found in each file. All files are compressed with bzip2.
